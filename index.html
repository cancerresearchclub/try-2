Play to raise money!

How will I raise money while playing a game?

When you are on this website, or playing the 2048 game, you are also viewing the advertizements on my page at the same time. The more you play, the more you view the advertizement, and the more money is paid to my website. All of the money earned by this website will be donated to a cancer research organization(to be chosen) by the end of each year. While you are having fun playing a game, you are also contibuting to a good cause!

I got the idea to create a fundraising game after seeing so many of my friends go play 2048 on their computer when they were bored. Why not play with a positive impact!

Volunteering OpportunityApril 7, 2019

Do you need volunteer hours here at the school? This is the right opportunity for you. Every Wednesday after school is the Cancer Ribbon Making!

The group of volunteers makes ribbons out of the resources provided to us by the Canadian Cancer Society. Once the ribbons are finished, the organization will use them to raise money during its runs and gatherings.
